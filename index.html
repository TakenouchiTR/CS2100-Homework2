Welcome to Petoskey, Michigan

Petoskey, Michigan is an upscale resort community that is nestled along the shores of Lake Michigan's Little Traverse Bay. People come to Petoskey to enjoy year-round re creational opportunities. The summer offers comfortable lake breezes and spectacular sunsets along gorgeous beaches, while winter brings fresh, abundant snow for skiing and snowmobiling. Known for its Million Dollar Sunsets, the summer home of Ernest Hemingway in his early years, and the Petoskey Stone, there is plenty to enjoy in Petoskey as a visitor or lifelong resident.
