Welcome to Petoskey, Michigan

A Four Season Community

Overlooking the shores of Lake Michigan's Little Traverse Bay in the northern tip of Michigan's Lower Peninsula you'll find Petoskey Michigan. A quaint resort community with unmatched beauty and charm, Petoskey has been a destination for more than 100-years. People return to soak in the rich views, breathe the clean air, eat at world-class restaurants and shop in the specialty shops and boutiques of its historic downtown.

As you plan your vacation in and around Petoskey, please feel free to use the abundant information on our web site to discover information for Petoskey, Walloon Lake, Harbor Springs, Alanson, and Pellston. You can learn about the famous Petoskey stone and why Ernest Hemingway proudly called this area home for so many years. You'll also learn about the great outdoor recreation opportunities that include bike trails, cross country ski trails, boating, fishing, and of course the Midwest's best downhill skiing. You'll find information about the famous resort communities of historic Bay View and the more recently developed Bay Harbor.

Welcome to Petoskey. If the site doesn't fully address your needs, we hope that you'll contact us so that we can help you collect all of the information you are seeking.
